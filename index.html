We give ways!
JuanDirection is a web application that will give you directions for commuting in the Metro.
We will give you ways that might keep you in time.
We will give you ways wherein you might save money.
We will give you ways to help you prevent getting lost.

Less Commuting Time. More Time for Others.

JuanDirection will TAKE YOU HOME faster with the provided estimated commuting time. The given estimated commuting time does not only depend on the distance that will be travelled but traffic will also be used as a basis.

Less Fare. More Savings.

JuanDirection will give you ONE WAY OR ANOTHER which can help you choose the cheapest way. An estimated fare for your journey is provided together with the estimated commuting time.

Less Confusions. More Efficient.

JuanDirection will help you enjoy the BEST SONG EVER while commuting. The detailed and comprehensive directions are what we will provide. So worry no more cause will guide you to the Metro.
